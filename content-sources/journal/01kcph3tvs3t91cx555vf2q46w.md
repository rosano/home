[Destigmatize being dumb](https://defenderofthebasic.substack.com/p/destigmatize-being-dumb)

> Any system that rewards finding flaws will improve. Apple, Google, etc will pay you a lot of money if you can find a security flaw in any of their systems. The military does this with matters of life & death _(if admitting failure is punished, people hide failure)_. If you find an inefficiency in the economy, you can make a lot of money fixing it _(through betting on the stock market, or starting a business)_.
