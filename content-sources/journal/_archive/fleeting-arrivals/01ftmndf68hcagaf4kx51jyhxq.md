[Amit Trivedi: Shubhaarambh](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7DpA6Imc97M)

From _Kai Po Che !_ (2013). Soundtrack for a Bollywood film, with lyrics in Gujarati. The introduction has a strong time feel without any rhythmic accompaniment. There’s bagpipes (!), and I know how odd it might be to feature that in Indian music, but the duduk-like instrument called ‘shehnai’ went right to my heart.
