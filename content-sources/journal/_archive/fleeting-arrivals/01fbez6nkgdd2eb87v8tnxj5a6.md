[Junior Braguinha Quinteto: Goonies](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P0tMd17j_T8)

From a set of live quintet recordings that could probably go together as an album, but seems impossible to assemble together without creating a YouTube account or relying on autoplay, so here’s just two tracks: [_Goonies_](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P0tMd17j_T8) (2017) mixes a high-octane bass solo with ambient noise (emphasis on noise) from the keyboardist—reminds me of Robert Glasper’s _Black Radio_. [_Brisa_](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0bqnFlPIZ5o) (2018) is hard-driving and odd-metered with harmonies that shift like mechanical gears.
