[Michel Freidenson: Notas no Ar (2011)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_e5P-SClE1Y)

Gave me a strong signal from the first few notes of the album. It features traditional Brazilian rhythms and instruments mixed with a fresher modern jazz sound. _Roda_ mixes samba and speedy bossa nova with a rare combination of piano melody doubled by trombone; the forro inspired _Cosmic For All_ I can describe as energetic and alive; _Je Suis Desolé_ is a swingy and slightly disjunct blues featuring some stride piano in the intro.
