[Ondatrópica: 3 Reyes de la Terapia](https://ondatropica.bandcamp.com/track/reyes-de-la-terapia)

From _Ondatrópica_ (2012). Intense combination of beatboxing and breathing (or perhaps hyperventilating) with a repetitive pattern that seems like it’s performed live and not a copy/paste of the same audio. This backdrop makes a strange fusion with the Cumbia-style improvising accordion. Dark and interesting like chocolate 80% noir.
