[Lisa Hannigan: What’ll I Do](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TvwJMa5b1Qg&t=2s)

From _Passenger_ (2011). Starting hook got my attention right away. Bright, sunny vibes created with vocals, strings, bass, percussion, and clapping; some of it would normally feel cliché to me, but I enjoyed this. The constant offbeats create a deep swing effect: your body will move.
