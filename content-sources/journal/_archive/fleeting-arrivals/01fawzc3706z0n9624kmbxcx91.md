[Aeolians of Oakwood University: Sabbath Hymn](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xJAuHig9Kec)

When I heard Jacob Collier call this the “best choir on earth” last week, I went through some of their recordings. This one From _Aeolianology Acappella, Vol. 2_ (2015) is a good example of a sound that fills your insides with light, maybe raising your shoulders too. The church chorale and organ textures with jazz voice-leadings on an a cappella album is a testament to the power of the human voice. They also did a variation on Take 6’s classic arrangement of _Get Away Jordan_.
