[Ron Everett: Glitter of the City (1977)](https://jazzmanholygrailseries.bandcamp.com/album/glitter-of-the-city)

Traditional jazz and swing sounds with weird stuff, and I dug several tracks: Royal Walk_ with warbly instrumentals, loops, screeching, spoken word; _Tipsy Lady_’s blues/hip-hop and old school drums; _Pretty Little Girl_’s singing off-key Latin vibe; the 8-bit bossa nova of _Untitled No. 4_.
