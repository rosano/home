[Hundred Waters: Particle](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PEkng2Un5n8)

From _Currency_ (2017). Starts with delicate acoustic piano tones and chords. I usually find it interesting when to use blocky (predictable) patterns from techno music without the music feeling too repetitive; the constant contrast here makes it always dynamic and alive. Lots of triplets and dotted rhythms throughout, twice used in the melody to create this feeling of continuously moving, dropping (for example, [around 57s](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PEkng2Un5n8&t=53s)). The lyrics and melody help evoke a sense of flying, soaring, falling.
