[The Halluci Nation: Electric Pow Wow Drum](https://thehallucination.bandcamp.com/track/electric-pow-wow-drum)

From _A Tribe Called Red_ (2013). There’s already so much power in traditional indigenous chanting, to add blasting electronic synths and amplification gives it just a little more oomph. Possible to tastefully connect such far away aesthetics. Super simple form, but interesting timbres from metal shakers and leather-skinned drums.
