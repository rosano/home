[Music from Africa Vol. 2 Shangaan Traditional / Sotho Chant (2015)](https://nyaminyamirecords.bandcamp.com/album/music-from-africa-vol-2-shangaan-traditional-sotho-chant)

Compilation album filled with South African music from the 1980s, mostly disco, except for these first two tracks: _Crestina_ and _Alexandra_’s triadic pentatonic harmonies and vocal percussion combines moves you from side to side.
