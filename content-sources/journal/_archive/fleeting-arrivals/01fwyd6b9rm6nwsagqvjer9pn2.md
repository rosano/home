[Vulfpeck: Back Pocket](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UZPX9KQbwsg)

From _Live at Madison Square Garden_ (2019). Inspiring to hear a thousand-strong audience sing a complex line in three part harmony. Lots of beatboxing, music via voice and the body. Guitar shredding partially mirrored on voice. Surprising to clarinets at the end in this context. Good vibes, happy times, saudades for live music.
