[Ayyuka: Maslak Halayı (2020)](https://ayyuka.bandcamp.com/album/maslak-halay)

Unapologetic and seamless weaving of psychedelic rock, Turkish scales and melodies, prepared electronic ambience and live instruments. Pay attention to the effects, the intensity without ‘loudness’, the microtonal inflections played on seemingly ordinary electric guitar. Tight drumming and a badass vibe throughout the album. My favourites: [_Maslak Halayı_](https://ayyuka.bandcamp.com/track/maslak-halay) fills me with power; [_Yukadans_](https://ayyuka.bandcamp.com/track/yukadans) is alive, helps body move, cool harmonizing between the two leads near the end; [_Komalı_](https://ayyuka.bandcamp.com/track/komal)’s got that pitch bending that I love from some kinds of Arab music; [_Ah Be Baba_](https://ayyuka.bandcamp.com/track/ah-be-baba) is an adventure filled with sudden turns and odd meters.
