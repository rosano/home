[Lingua Ignota: PENNSYLVANIA FURNACE](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7YRMV7ffPpY)

From _SINNER GET READY_ (2021). Filled with dark piano textures, and a strong, pure vocal tone that manages to create this deep and expansive intensity with just a few parts. Reminds me of how powerful the acoustic piano can be. You can hear the pedals of the piano triggering overtones…
