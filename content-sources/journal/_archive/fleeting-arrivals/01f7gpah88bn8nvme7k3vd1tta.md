[Fabrice Koffy, Marika Galea, Michael Go: Le Soir](https://mtlsoundresistance.bandcamp.com/track/le-soir-fabrice-koffy-marika-galea-michael-go)

From _Montréal Sound Resistance: Chapitre I_ (2021) builds from French spoken word to trio music, to an improvised duo with a lovely African triplet feel. The bilingual album of poetry and spoken word is made in Montreal and dedicated to the memory of George Floyd.
