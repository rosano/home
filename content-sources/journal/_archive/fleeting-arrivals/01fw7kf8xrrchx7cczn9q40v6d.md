[Zventa Sventana: Мужа дома нету](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9tY4OK-Dg_s)

From _Мужа дома нету_ (2019). Combines electronic sounds with folk singing. Lots of cool percussion and odd production details throughout to keep things dynamic (several only occurring once in the whole song). Touches elements of techno music without being too formulaic, might be the combination with various vocal timbres that keeps it feeling alive.
