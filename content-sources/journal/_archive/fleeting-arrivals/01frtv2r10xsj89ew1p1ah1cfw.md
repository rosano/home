[Bobbie Gentry: Reunion](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xBl4YQMoBKk)

From _The Delta Sweete_ (1968). Complex interleaving of multiple parts with folksy Southern USA accents. Listen to the percussive clapping, bass drum, and guitar on the left channel, with voice and trumpets in the right channel. Reminds me of Moondog.
