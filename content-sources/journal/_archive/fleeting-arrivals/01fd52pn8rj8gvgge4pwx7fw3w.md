[Nihiloxica: Kaloli (2020)](https://nihiloxica.bandcamp.com/album/kaloli)

Shiny little gem mixing traditional Ugandan drumming with elements of electronic music and techno. _Supuki_’s dark beats and groove drumming, combines the electronic with an earthy sound—intense; _Tewali Sukali_’s body-shaking rhythms are accompanied by grungy noise; _Gunjula_ has fast-driving multi-layered polyrhythms; _Busoga_’s the lead synths are dripping light all over the percussion; _Kaloli_ surprised me with its thrashing metal.
