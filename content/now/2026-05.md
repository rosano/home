Still in Berlin; great to hang out a while here. Mostly working on projects, doing capoeira. Still less social than what I'm used to and starting to consider how much longer I want to stay in that mode.

Happy to introduce [Abendbrot](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Abendbrot) ("evening bread" with stuff on it) into my routines; greatly simplifies 'figuring out what to eat'.

Taking far less photos means less photo management, and I'm enjoying that.

Noticing when I'm [appeasing](https://www.tiktok.com/@watchfulcoyote/video/7634632780743216397) and trying to root this out of my relationships, which raises the bar for how good and clear I would like to feel with people in my circles.

<roco-divider></roco-divider>

My attention has recently shifted away from social media. In addition to avoiding [scrolling memes](https://rosano.ca/blog/where-am-i-scrolling/), I tuned out of tweets, or what I call "high-frequency chatter". Notifications are now my "feed", and I'll happily engage with people about what I post, but my following attention is now mostly on those who publish slower on their own websites.
